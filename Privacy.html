Traject collects information about you in several ways: information you directly provide to us, information we collect automatically when you use or access our Services, information collected from unaffiliated third parties or sources, and by using and combining personal data to derive additional personal data from or about you.

The types of information we may collect include:

  • Identifiers, such as your name, email address, postal address, online identifier, account name, and phone number;

  • Commercial information, such as product wish lists, order history, or marketing preferences;
  • Internet or other electronic network activity information, including browsing history, search history, and information regarding a consumer's interaction with an internet website, application, or advertisement;
  • Information about your business such as company name, size and type;
  • Demographic information such as your age, education, gender, interests, and zip code;
  • Payment information such as your credit information, credit card, debit card, or bank account number, other financial information, or communications preferences;

Information About Your Use of the Services

Usage Information

We collect information about your use of the Services, such as the search terms you enter, the pages and messages you click, etc.

Automatically Collected Information

When you access or use our Services, we automatically collect information about you, including:

  • Log Information: We collect log information about your use of the Services, including the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address, and the website you visited before and after navigating to our Services.

Web beacons are electronic images that may be used in our Services or emails and help deliver cookies, count visits and understand usage and marketing campaign effectiveness. For more information about cookies and how to disable them, please see "Your Choices" below and our Cookie Policy.
